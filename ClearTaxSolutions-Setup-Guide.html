Verify Your Domain with Google
Google needs to confirm you own the domain. They'll give you a TXT record to add.
  1. Google will display a TXT verification record (looks like: google-site-verification=abc123...)
  2. Log in to your domain registrar (where you bought the domain)
  3. Go to DNS settings
  4. Add a new TXT record:
    — Host/Name: @
    — Value: paste the verification string Google gave you
    — TTL: default (or 3600)
  5. Go back to Google Workspace and click "Verify"
  6. May take up to 48 hours to propagate, but usually works within 15 minutes
Set Up MX Records for Email
This tells the internet to route emails to Google's servers so you can send/receive from joseph@cleartaxsolutions.com.
  1. In your domain registrar DNS settings, add these MX records:

5ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM
5ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM

Deploy Your Website on Vercel
15 Minutes — Free Hosting
Connect GitHub Repo to Vercel FREE
  1. Go to vercel.com and click "Sign Up"
  2. Sign up with your GitHub account (the one that owns addyanddaddy/ClearTaxSolutions)
  3. Click "New Project"
  4. Select the ClearTaxSolutions repository
  5. Vercel auto-detects Next.js — leave all settings as default
  6. Click "Deploy"
  7. Wait ~90 seconds — your site will be live at cleartaxsolutions.vercel.app
Connect Your Custom Domain
  1. In Vercel dashboard, go to your project → Settings → Domains
  2. Type cleartaxsolutions.com and click Add
  3. Vercel will show you DNS records to add. Go to your domain registrar's DNS settings:
  4. Add an A record:
    — Host/Name: @
    — Value: 76.76.21.21 (Vercel's IP)
  5. Add a CNAME record:
    — Host/Name: www
    — Value: cname.vercel-dns.com
  6. Go back to Vercel and click Verify
  7. SSL certificate is automatic and free — HTTPS is enforced immediately
ℹ️ DNS propagation can take up to 48 hours, but usually works within 30 minutes. Your site will be accessible at both cleartaxsolutions.com and www.cleartaxsolutions.com.

Set Up Square for Payments
20 Minutes
Create Square Account FREE — Pay Per Transaction
Square charges 2.9% + 30¢ per online transaction. No monthly fees. No minimums.
  1. Go to squareup.com and click "Get Started"
  2. Sign up with your email (use joseph@cleartaxsolutions.com after Workspace is set up)
  3. Enter business information:
    — Business Name: Clear Tax Solutions
    — Business Type: Individual/Sole Proprietor (or LLC if registered)
    — Industry: Financial Services → Tax Preparation
    — EIN or SSN for tax reporting
  4. Link your bank account for deposits (transfers arrive in 1-2 business days, or instant for a fee)
  5. Verify your identity (government ID required)
Get Your Square API Credentials
These are needed to connect Square to the client portal for online payments.
  1. Go to developer.squareup.com
  2. Sign in with your Square account
  3. Click "+" → New Application
  4. Name it: Clear Tax Solutions Portal
  5. You'll get two sets of credentials — Sandbox (for testing) and Production (for real payments)
  6. Copy and save these (you'll give them to your developer):
    — Application ID (starts with sq0idp-)
    — Access Token (starts with EAAAl...)
    — Location ID (found under Locations in your Square Dashboard)
⚠️ NEVER share your Production Access Token publicly or put it in emails. Only share it directly with your developer through a secure channel.
Set Up Square Invoices (Can Use Immediately)
Even before the portal integration is live, you can send invoices through Square directly.
  1. In your Square Dashboard, go to Invoices
  2. Click "Create Invoice"
  3. Add client info, line items, and amount (based on case complexity)
  4. Send directly to client's email — they can pay with card, Apple Pay, or Google Pay
  5. Square tracks all payments, sends receipts automatically, and handles tax reporting

What We Need From You to Connect Square to the Portal

  • Application ID — from developer.squareup.com
  • Access Token (Production) — share securely, NOT via email
  • Location ID — from Square Dashboard → Locations
  • Confirmation that your bank account is linked and verified
